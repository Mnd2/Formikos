Transporto paslaugos

For your convenience we carry out multimodal cargo transportation, which allows significantly cut the cost of transportation and delivery time. By directly controlling cargo transportation, we promptly prepare documents in accordance with the applicable requirements. We work with countries such as AUSTRALIA, INDIA, TURKEY, RUSSIA AND UKRAINE, we transport cargo in EU directions. We can always find alternatives to other countries. Our company provides quality services and we know what our customer needs. Reputation and long-term relationships with partners are very important to us, therefore we always try to justify expectations of our clients and be professional in our field.

Sandėliavimo paslaugos

We will take care of the storage of your cargo in both ordinary and customs warehouses and will help to arrange all the documents required for the import or export of the cargo. For goods warehousing and logistics, our company's warehouses are located in Lithuania, Germany, Russia and Ukraine. The warehouses are equipped with modern equipment, so we can quickly and safely load and unload cargo.

Juridinės paslaugos

Legal issues that concern you will be dealt with by lawyers with relevant legal knowledge and competencies. In the case of a complex problem involving two or more areas of law, it will always be possible to refer the matter to a legal expert in the area concerned. We provide legal services in Russia.

Muitinės tarpininkai

Customs broker services in Lithuania, Russia and Ukraine. Completion of import, export, transit declarations. Representation of the client in customs during import, transit and other procedures, advising clients on cargo declaration and customs clearance.

Krovinio draudimas

Cargo insurance - a type of insurance when cargo transported in a vehicle is insured against damage, destruction and theft during transportation. Cargo insurance cover shall take effect from the moment the insured cargo is transported for the carriage specified in the insurance contract from the place where it was previously stored and shall expire as soon as the cargo is delivered and unloaded at the destination, specified in the cargo insurance contract. Cargo insurance coverage is valid during intermediate storage.
